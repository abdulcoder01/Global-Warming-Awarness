Global Warming Awareness

Learn about the effects of global warming and how we can take action.

What is Global Warming?

Global warming is the gradual increase in the Earth's temperature caused by the buildup of greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide. These gases trap heat in the atmosphere, leading to various environmental challenges.

Effects of Global Warming

  • Melting glaciers and rising sea levels
  • More frequent extreme weather events
  • Destruction of ecosystems and habitats
  • Impact on agriculture and food security

How Can You Help?

Reducing your carbon footprint can make a difference. Here are some ways you can help fight global warming:

  • Use public transportation or carpool
  • Reduce, reuse, and recycle
  • Conserve energy at home
  • Support renewable energy sources
  • Plant trees and support reforestation efforts
